# Sample Medical Questions

The following examples demonstrate the types of medical questions that PerMed is designed to process.

---

## Example 1

**Question**

What are the common symptoms of diabetes?

**Expected Output**

A medically accurate explanation describing symptoms such as increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision, and unexplained weight loss.

---

## Example 2

**Question**

What should I do if I have a persistent fever?

**Expected Output**

An explanation of possible causes, self-care recommendations, warning signs, and advice to consult a healthcare professional if symptoms persist.

---

## Example 3

**Question**

Can high blood pressure increase the risk of stroke?

**Expected Output**

A medical explanation describing the relationship between hypertension and stroke risk with appropriate clinical recommendations.

---

## Example 4

**Question**

What are the side effects of Ibuprofen?

**Expected Output**

A concise explanation including common and serious side effects, precautions, and contraindications.

---

## Example 5

**Question**

How can Type 2 Diabetes be prevented?

**Expected Output**

Evidence-based lifestyle recommendations including healthy diet, regular exercise, weight management, and routine medical checkups.

---

## Disclaimer

PerMed is developed for research and educational purposes only.

It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
